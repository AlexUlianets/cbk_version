About

Oplao is a new type of weather service. Oplao provides definitely live weather by using perfect weather data model connected with Web 3D technology. You can imagine tomorrow weather by discovering one of live themes in the Oplao Gallery. Oplao provides global weather forecast and weather content for websites, businesses and travel industry. We specialize in worldwide City and Town, Sailing, Hill and Mountain, Ski and Sports weather. We cover over 2 million worldwide cities. Our weather forecast is trusted and used from SME's to large corporate clients.

Our team members are based in UK, PL and US. Our high-tech weather data center are situated in US. Unlike other weather provider we have developed our own weather forecasting model which could deliver reliable and accurate weather information for any geo-point in the world. Our weather model is run along with other metrological models to compare and deliver accurate weather forecast.

We also derive our weather data for research and training purposes from renowned establishments like European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts, World Meteorological Organization, NASA weather satellite imagery, NOAA GFS2 model and JMA model.
